The Amazing Henry Ford!

Henry Ford (1863-1947), founder of the Ford Motor Company, revolutionized the automotive industry with assembly line production, lowering costs and democratizing car ownership. The Model T, introduced in 1908, exemplified his impact. Ford's innovative mass production methods transformed industrial manufacturing, and his commitment to worker welfare set new standards, including a five-day workweek and higher wages.
